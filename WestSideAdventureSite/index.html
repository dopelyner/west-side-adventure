Welcome to West Side Adventure game

America has always been known as the land of opportunity, the land of the free and the land of the brave. They only forgot to mention that all of these adjectives and dreams had a dependency - being white. Even so, people of colour and those in precarious conditions will always fight for a better life. And just like in the song America, from the musical West Side Story, there are a lot of adversities and inequalities that one must face and overcome in order to make it in the US (or anywhere else, really). Start your journey in a 100% original pixel-art action game that aims to depict, in a metaphorical fashion, some of the hardships that immigrants leaving Puerto Rico must face while trying to reach the US.
